## Cookie compliance requirements

Implement a production-ready cookie consent and privacy preference system for the public site and app.

Requirements:
- detect and categorize all cookies and trackers as:
  - strictly necessary
  - analytics
  - preferences
  - marketing
- block all non-essential cookies and scripts until valid consent is obtained in jurisdictions that require prior consent
- show a consent banner with:
  - Accept All
  - Reject Non-Essential
  - Manage Preferences
- provide a persistent “Cookie Preferences” link in the footer and legal area
- allow users to withdraw or update consent as easily as they gave it
- log/store consent choices with timestamp, policy version, and consent categories
- integrate privacy-policy and cookie-policy disclosures with the actual cookies/scripts used
- honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where applicable
- if Google Analytics/Ads/GTM are used, implement Consent Mode with denied defaults until consent is granted
- ensure strictly necessary cookies can still function without requiring opt-in
- make the banner accessible by keyboard and screen readers
- make consent behavior region-aware where practical, especially for EU/EEA and California visitors
- do not load analytics, marketing, chat tracking, or similar third-party scripts before consent if they are non-essential
- add a dedicated Cookie Policy page or a clearly labeled cookie section within the Privacy Policy
- ensure the floating chat widget respects consent if it sets non-essential cookies or tracking technologies
